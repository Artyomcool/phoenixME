QA Methodology and Testing Strategy

A key goal of the Mobile & Embedded open source core projects is to establish quality methodologies, strategies, and criteria that ensure all contributions are of high quality and easily tested. To meet this goal, the following guidelines apply:

  • Quality experts must be involved early in the development phases and continuously throughout development to ensure quality and ease of testing is designed and built into the contributions.

  • Contributors are required to write unit tests, and functional tests where applicable, for their own code. They are also required to test their code before the code review.

  • Extensive testing—including unit, smoke, integration, and build—will be executed before committing changes to the repository and on a regular basis to ensure that only quality contributions are committed and that problems are caught early.

  • Quality experts advise contributors on testing strategies and issues such as test methodology, coverage, design, and results.

  • The QA team at Sun Microsystems, Inc. will continue to develop, maintain, and perform system integration, regression, and release testing for all releases.
